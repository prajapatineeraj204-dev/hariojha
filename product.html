Product Description

Our Performance Air Filter is designed to deliver maximum airflow with superior filtration. The multi-layer cotton gauze construction allows for increased air volume while trapping harmful contaminants that can damage your engine.

Unlike disposable paper filters, our performance filter is washable and reusable, making it an eco-friendly and cost-effective solution. With proper maintenance, this filter can last the lifetime of your vehicle.

Installation is simple and requires no special tools. Most users report noticeable improvements in throttle response and engine sound within minutes of installation.

Installation Guide

Step 1: Locate your vehicle's air filter box. This is typically a black plastic box near the front of the engine compartment.

Step 2: Open the air filter box by releasing the clips or removing the screws that hold it together.

Step 3: Remove the old air filter and clean any debris from the air filter box.

Step 4: Place the new performance air filter in the box, ensuring it sits properly in the housing.

Step 5: Close the air filter box and secure the clips or screws.

Note: No tools are required for most installations. The entire process typically takes 5-10 minutes.
